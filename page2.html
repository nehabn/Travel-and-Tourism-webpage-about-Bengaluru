Bannerghatta National Park

About...

Bannerghatta National Park is a sanctuary for a large variety of flora and fauna. The main attractions here are an aquarium, a zoo, a Children's park, Crocodile Farm, Snake Park and a Prehistoric Animals' Park. It is also popular for hiking and trekking. Bannerghatta Biological Park consists of the Bannerghatta Zoo, the Safari, the Butterfly Park and the Rescue and Rehabilitation centre.


Location : Bannerghatta
Distance: 23 km from city centre
The nearest landmark to Bannerghatta National Park is Jayadeva Hospital or Mico checkpoint, which is closest on a bus route via Ring Road. Hop on a city bus from Shivajinagar Bus Stand, City market, or Kempegowda Bus Station which are available in a gap of 20 minutes, and reach the destination in less than an hour.
