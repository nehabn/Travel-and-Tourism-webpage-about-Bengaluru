Lumbini gardens

About...

Lumbini Gardens is a public park on the banks of the Nagawara . It contains an eco-friendly boating park and a 12,500 square foot artificial beach and children's pool. A one of its kind water-front leisure & eco-friendly park, the Lumbini Gardens is unique in its concept and one of the favourites of the locals. From water rides to boating and beautiful landscaping, you can indulge in your favourite activity with friends and family. It can accommodate up to 5,000 visitors. Also, this park offers the option of holding get-togethers and parties. It includes some fun boating activities, amusement parks , and pool is also present.


Location : Outer Ring road , Nagawara
Distance : 2.8 km from city centre
